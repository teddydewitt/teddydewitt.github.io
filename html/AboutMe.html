About Me




Prior to starting my graduate program in 2013, I worked for many years in the financial industry. From 2012 to 2013 I worked as a financial analyst in the Office of Financial Strategy and Planning at Harvard University. From 2009 to 2012, I worked as a credit analyst at Standard and Poor’s Ratings Services focusing on a credit portfolio of project-financed power plant assets. Prior to that I received an MBA from the Yale School of Management (SOM), where I focused on Finance and Organizational Behavior. Before heading to Yale SOM for business school, I worked as a content manager at FactSet Research Systems.


I currently live in Ann Arbor, MI with my wife Alison and young daughter. In my spare time… well I spend most of my time - spare and otherwise - trying to keep up with my daughter! In the few moments I have left after that, I try to stay musical. I have led a kids’ song circle for the residential graduate student family community. I have participated in the fantastically titled Michigan Ross student a cappella group, the CEOhs. And on occasion, I have been known to sing and play the guitar at special occasions.


I am originally from Canton, Ohio, but I try to keep that Ohio stuff under wraps during my time in the Mitten.
